INFORMATION

 

The Graffiti-Free NYC Program removes illicit markings across the five boroughs.


Graffiti-Free NYC is a cooperative effort among the NYC Economic Development Corporation, the DSNY, and the Office of the Mayor. For more information, see here.


DSNY-GRAFFITI-TRACKING-MANHATTAN displays coordinates of graffiti incidences in Manhattan from January 1, 2022, to January 5, 2022, and their corresponding resolutions.


To view the complete DSNY Graffiti Tracking dataset, see here.
